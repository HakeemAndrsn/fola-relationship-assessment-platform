# Site Standards (permanent)

These standards apply to all pages on LOVEBETTER.co.za. Do not regress them in future work.

## Payment Pages (`/individual-assessment`, `/assessment`)

- Must include a "What's included" recap box (3 bullet points) above the customer form
- Must include a security/privacy reassurance line below the payment button
- Must include a human sign-off: "You're in the right place. — The FOLA Team"
- Meta description must match the assessment type (individual vs couples)

## Blog / The Uncommon Practice

- Every article page must include an assessment CTA between the comments section and the discovery call CTA
- The blog index must include an assessment CTA above the newsletter signup
- Every article must have a single `<h1>` (the title), with `<h2>` subheadings
- All `<h2>` subheadings must have an `id` attribute (slugified from heading text) for anchor links
- Every article page must have Open Graph + Twitter card metadata

## Warm-Traffic CTA

- A sticky CTA bar must appear after scrolling past the hero on the landing page
- Must link to the discovery call (calendly.com/folasessions/discovery-call)
- Must be hidden on initial load (translate-y-full), slide up on scroll

## General SEO

- All pages must have unique, descriptive meta titles and descriptions
- Open Graph and Twitter card metadata on all content pages
- Slugs must be keyword-rich, hyphenated, and human-readable
